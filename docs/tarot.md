# Fortune's Veil

A quiet, single-player tarot reading. Sit with the Reader, ask one question, and
let a three-card spread answer it.

- **Players:** 1 (a bot "Reader" is seated automatically — there's nothing to
  invite or wait for)
- **Goal:** none. It's a reflective reading, not a contest.

---

## How it works

1. **Open the room** from the lobby. You'll land in a small waiting room with the
   Reader already at the table — just press **Start Game**.
2. **Name yourself and ask a question.** Both of these become the seed for the
   shuffle, so the *same name and question always draw the same cards*. Change
   either one for a different reading.
3. **Flip the spread one card at a time.** Each card is dealt to a position and
   read in turn:

   | Position | Meaning |
   | --- | --- |
   | **Past** | What has shaped the situation. |
   | **Present** | Where things stand right now. |
   | **Future** | Where the current path leads. |

4. **Read the full stack.** Once all three are turned over, the complete reading
   is laid out together. Start a **New reading** to ask again.

---

## The cards

The deck is the 22 **Major Arcana** — the iconic, archetypal cards (The Fool, The
Tower, The Star, and so on). Each can land **upright** or **reversed**:

- **Upright** — the card's plain, forward meaning.
- **Reversed** — its shadow or blocked meaning. Roughly two in five cards land
  reversed.

Interpretations are intentionally broad, so any card reads plausibly against any
question. Take what's useful and leave the rest.

---

## Notes

- There is no chat, no invite, and no timer — the reading moves entirely at your
  pace.
- Because the cards are tied to your exact wording, jot down a question you like
  if you want to return to the same reading later.
